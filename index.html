    About Me

    I'm a software developer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and over 3 years of experience in Unity and C#.


    My university experience has given me a solid foundation in Software Engineering, AI, GUI Design, Databases, Web Development and Compiler Design.


    My journey in game development began with Scratch in high school and has since evolved into creating two full games in Unity. One of these, Shape-Shift Snacker, won first place at the NUCC-FCUP 2024 Game Jam. Now, I've been working on a much larger project for over 2 years.


    Beyond programming, I've been producing music for over 7 years. After releasing several tracks, I won first place in the Call Out Remix competition by Dimatis in April 2022. Now, I'm using the experience I've gained to create the soundtrack for my current game project.
