    About Me

    I'm a passionate software developer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and over three years of experience in Unity and C#. My journey in game development started with Scratch, and has since evolved into creating two full games in Unity, with a third, much larger project currently in development.


    My friend and I won first place with our game Shape-Shift Snacker at the NUCC-FCUP 2024 Game Jam, a collaboration that reinforced my passion for creativity and teamwork.


    While game development is my primary focus, my university experience has given me a solid foundation in Software Engineering, AI, GUI Design, Databases, Web Development and Compiler Design. I'm eager to expand my expertise and tackle new challenges in future roles.


    Beyond programming, I love producing music. After several releases, I won first place in the Call Out Remix competition by Dimatis and Baiza in April 2022. I'm now applying the knowledge I've gained over the years to create the soundtrack for my current game project.


    At my core, I am a creative problem-solver with a passion for software development, game design, and music production. I'm always eager to take on new challenges and push the boundaries of what I can create.
