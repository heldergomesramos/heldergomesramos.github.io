Pato em Fradelos

[Game and Website are in Development]

Note: Current name of the game is a placeholder.


Pato em Fradelos is a beautifully pixelated 2D metroidvania adventure where your evolving understanding of the environment reveals hidden possibilities that were always there, just waiting to be perceived.

- Master difficult but fair platforming challenges.
- Explore a vast, interconnected world, uncover secrets, and solve thought-provoking puzzles.
- Learn the behaviors of the creatures and the environment around you, and use this knowledge to overcome obstacles and uncover hidden paths.
- Solve the mysteries of an abandoned island and the enigmatic black matter that pervades it.
- Evolve with powerful new skills and abilities.
